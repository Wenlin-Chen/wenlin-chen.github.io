About Me

I’m a PhD student in Machine Learning at University of Cambridge and Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems under the Cambridge-Tübingen PhD Fellowship, advised by Prof. José Miguel Hernández-Lobato and Prof. Bernhard Schölkopf.

I’m keen on basic research in machine learning and its scientific applications. My research interest lies at the intersection of deep learning and probabilistic methods for generative modeling and sampling. For an overview of my research, please visit my publication page or Google Scholar profile.
